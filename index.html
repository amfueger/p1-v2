How to play

Step 1: Move and Attack

On your turn, examine and choose one of the two Move cards in front of you. Then, move one of your pawns as shown on that card, with the location of the pawn corresponding to the black square on the card.

Step 2: Exchange Cards

Your previously used Move card is placed on the side of the board while you add the previous side card to your hand.

How do I win?

You either capture your opponent's Master pawn, or you move your own Master pawn to the opponent's Temple Arch space.

What if I don't want to move?

If you have a legal move, you must take it, even if it traps you or otherwise ends up with your losing a pawn. If you absolutely do not have a legal move, you may pass, but still switch cards with the side card.

It is Reds turn!

Side Card

Use this when you have picked a pawn/card combo that has no legal move with any cards. Forfeit turn, pay better attention next time!
